treasure

Reading:

On-Yomi: ホウ — Kun-Yomi: たから

Heisig story:

Every house has its treasure, as every thief knows only too well. While the things we treasure most are usually of sentimental value, we take the original sense of the term treasure here and make it refer to jewels kept in one's house.
