father

Reading:

On-Yomi: フ — Kun-Yomi: ちち

Heisig story:

The kindness and hard work of the ideal father is seen in this abbreviation of the taskmaster that leaves off his rod or whip (the first stroke) and replaces it with the sweat of the father's brow (the two drops at the top).
