stare

Reading:

On-Yomi: チョウ — Kun-Yomi: なが.める

Heisig story:

To give someone the "evil eye" is to stare at them, wishing them evil. The roots of the superstition are old and almost universal throughout the cultures of the world. In this kanji, too, being stared at is depicted as an eye that portends evil.
