Reading:

On-Yomi: シ — Kun-Yomi: わたくし、わたし

Heisig story:

Wheat . . . elbow. Like the characters for I (Frame 17) and ego (Frame 690), this kanji is also representative of the subject, with the special connotation of privacy.
