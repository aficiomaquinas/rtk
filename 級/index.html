5) [taikura] 14-1-2009(20): Class, grade, rank. Members of a particular class or rank are connected (as if by a thread) by their similarities and common circumstances; and often, by the extent to which they will reach out and exert themselves to new heights. 下級 (low grade). 学級 (school class). 勤労階級 (working class). 最高級 (top class). 審級 (instance). 上級 (advanced level, senior). 中級 (intermediate level). 飛び級 (skipping a grade). 上の級に進む (to move up to a higher grade).
