Africa

Reading:

On-Yomi: ア、オ — Kun-Yomi: おもね.る

Heisig story:

This kanji, an abbreviation for Africa, is now used chiefly for its sound, "a," not unlike the kanji for Italy and the sound "i" that we met earlier (Frame 1245). Its composite elements are: pinnacles . . . can.
