spiny

Reading:

On-Yomi: コウ、キョウ — Kun-Yomi: ふさぐ、やまにれ、おおむね

Heisig story:

This character refers originally to a deciduous, rough tree that grows on mountain plains. From this it gets the secondary sense of rugged or spiny. Its primitive elements: tree . . . grow late.
