Reading:

On-Yomi: ソウ、シュ、シュウ — Kun-Yomi: さが.す

Heisig story:

Fingers . . . monkey . . . crotch.

Koohii stories:

1) [radical_tyro] 20-6-2007(204): The monkey searches around with his fingers for his crotch.
