Heisig story:

Acorn . . . saber.

Koohii stories:

1) [fuaburisu] 15-4-2008(199): This famous ninja (I always use ninjas with sabres, distinct from samurai) used to engrave the kanji for his name onto acorns, and throw the acorns to distract his enemies. 2) [rgravina] 3-8-2006(76): While it's traditional for lovers to engrave their names on a tree in many countries, in Japan, however, it was traditional for a samurai to signify his undying love for a woman by engraving the kanji of her name on an acorn with his sabre.
