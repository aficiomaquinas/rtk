desk

Reading:

On-Yomi: キ — Kun-Yomi: つくえ

Heisig story:

We need to fix imagination here on two things to learn the kanji for desk: the wonderful rough wood of which it has been hewn and the wind that blows across it, sending your papers flying all over the room. These two elements, written in that order, dictate how to write the character.
