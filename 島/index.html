island

Reading:

On-Yomi: トウ — Kun-Yomi: しま

Heisig story:

The bird's tail is tucked under here, because it has come to stop on a mountain to rest from its journey across the waters. Thus the kanji comes to mean an island.

Koohii stories:

1) [Immacolata] 7-5-2006(176): This island is the home of the terrible mythical bird Roc. It sits mighty atop the mountain waiting for the next hapless ship to pass by.

2) [mezbup] 8-7-2009(27): On the mountain of the island.. there were birds with no tail feathers.

3) [lifeflaw] 4-9-2009(17): From the bird's point of view, an island is a mountain in the middle of water and it is very suitable for resting its tired tail feathers.

4) [kazelee] 29-8-2008(6): Tweety bird looses his tail feathers and gets stuck on a mountainous Island.
