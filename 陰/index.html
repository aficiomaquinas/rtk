shade

Reading:

On-Yomi: イン — Kun-Yomi: かげ、かげ.る

Heisig story:

Just as the sunshine (Frame 1397) represents the masculine principle in nature (Yang), the shade stands for the feminine principle (Yin). Its elements are: pinnacle . . . clock . . . rising cloud.
