dispatch

Reading:

On-Yomi: ケン — Kun-Yomi: つか.う、-つか.い、-づか.い、つか.わす、や.る

Heisig story:

This kanji takes away the maestro's baton and replaces it with a purse. The road represents his being dispatched on his way a san obvious misfit. You will remember that when he did have his baton, he was being chased down the road by his fans. All of which shows what a difference a single stroke can make!
