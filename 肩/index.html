On-Yomi: ケン — Kun-Yomi: かた

Heisig story:

Door . . . flesh.

Koohii stories:

1) [shaydwyrm] 18-7-2007(281): The shoulder is the best part of the body to bash a door open with (Don't ever try this. Use your foot).

2) [dwhitman] 6-12-2007(108): The shoulder is part of the body you use to push through the noren in a doorway. I was having trouble with positioning of the primitives until I tweaked shaydwyrm's story slightly. If you think of pushing through a noren, this makes sense as a pictograph. The noren hangs from above, and you push through it from below.

3) [Raichu] 14-1-2006(24): A part of the body I always seem to bump against the door.

4) [fuaburisu] 8-12-2005(15): See those pieces of meat, on either sides of the door frame ? They are the shoulders of this body-builder who tried to get through …

5) [Viking101] 18-3-2012(5): My friend decided to act like superman and bust the door down with his shoulder. Needless to say, the end result was him crying and clutching the flesh of his tender shoulder.
