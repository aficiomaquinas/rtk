faint

Reading:

On-Yomi: ユウ、ヨウ — Kun-Yomi: ふか.い、かす.か、くら.い、しろ.い

Heisig story:

Two cocoons . . . mountain. Observe how the two vertical strokes of the mountain are extended upwards to serve as a kind of enclosure.

Koohii stories:

1) [dingomick] 20-12-2007(184): The villagers haven't the faintest idea that giant coccoons resting in the mountain are soon to hatch.

2) [greenapple] 24-5-2006(100): Frodo and Sam fainted in the mountains and the spider wrapped them in cacoons.

3) [szreter] 23-6-2008(72): Hint: this is faint as in dim, weak or indistinct, not the verb.

4) [CharleyGarrett] 5-11-2006(41): Two cocoons set out to climb a mountain, but they get separated. They shout to each other, but their voices, blocked by the mountain, are so faint. Notice that the first stroke of mountain is first, followed by the 2 cocoons, then the conclusion of mountain.

5) [theasianpleaser] 21-1-2009(35): Two people wrapped in their sleeping bags like cocoons are secluded in the mountains and can't hear the faintest sound.
