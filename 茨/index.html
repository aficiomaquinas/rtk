Reading:

On-Yomi: シ、ジ — Kun-Yomi: いばら、かや、くさぶき

Heisig story:

Earlier we made mention of the story of Briar Rose (or "Sleeping Beauty," as we called her in frame 165) and drew attention to the briar hedge that grew up all about her castle. But in the second part of the story, these briars blossomed into flowers. Hence her name, Briar Rose. Be careful not to confuse this character with that for thorn (Frame 446).
