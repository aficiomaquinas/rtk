Reading:

On-Yomi: セイ

Heisig story:

This kanji show a unique system for leading cows to the slaughterer's saber: one ties a belt about their waist and fixes that belt to an overhead cable, pulling the cow up into the air where it hangs suspended, helpless against the fate that awaits it.
