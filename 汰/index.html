cleanse

Reading:

On-Yomi: タ、タイ — Kun-Yomi: おご.る、にご.る、よな.げる

Heisig story:

This character can mean both to cleanse and to make dirty. We will choose the latter and imagine someone who is displeasingly plump going to a skinny spa whose medicinal waters promise to cleanse him of his unwanted corpulence. Picture him sitting in the spa as the pounds melt away, leaving a greasy scum on top of the water.
