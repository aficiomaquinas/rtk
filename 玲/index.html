Koohii stories:

1) [Katsuo] 12-6-2007(35): When the king barks out his orders, all the chandeliers and other ornaments in the palace start tinkling.

2) [sgrant] 23-8-2009(4): The king (having no real power) likes to give orders to his family jewels - "Commence tinkling!". (Thanks to bodhisamaya).

3) [mantixen] 27-7-2009(4): The king orders the tinkling of jewels to help him take a tinkle himself.

4) [decals] 20-12-2010(1): Royal orders from the King are read aloud by the town crier: "If you sprinkle when you tinkle, be a sweetie, wipe the seatie!" These orders would stand the test of time until they would appear carved in bathroom stalls in present day times.

5) [adrianbarritt] 5-3-2008(1): The king gives orders not to be disturbed whilst he's tinkling in the toilet.
