make a deal

Reading:

On-Yomi: ショウ — Kun-Yomi: あきな.う

Heisig story:

See the peddlar standing atop his motorcycle helmet as if it were a soapbox, hawking his wares to passersby. The legs and mouth represent the tools of the trade of making a deal any way you can.
