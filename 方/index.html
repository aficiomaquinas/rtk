direction

Reading:

On-Yomi: ホウ — Kun-Yomi: かた、-かた、-がた

Heisig story:

Spinning a dagger about on its hilt on the top of a top hat - waiting to see in which direction it points when it comes to rest - one leaves to fate where one is going next. Take care in writing this character.

Heisig comment:

* As a primitive, this character will take the sense of a compass, the instrument used to determine direction.
