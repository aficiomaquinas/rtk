nightfall

Reading:

On-Yomi: バン

Heisig story:

Sun . . . rabbit.

Koohii stories:

1) [nolusu] 9-9-2007(233): At nightfall the sun excuses itself and goes to sleep.

2) [Asriel] 13-7-2009(93): This is EASY!!! Japanese people see a RABBIT ON THE MOON (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Moon_rabbit ) and so nightfall is when the sun leaves to make way for the rabbit.

3) [fuaburisu] 5-2-2006(59): Imagine Bugs Bunny halfway sticking out of a rabbit hole, somewhere in Japan, taking in the sun, when all of a sudden, night falls. Our "sun-loving rabbit" burrows his way into the earth and pops out on the other side of the planet (say somewhere in America) and there it is, the sun high and bright in the sky! Thus, whenever it is nightfall you can be sure our "sun-loving rabbit" is taking in the sun somewhere else accross the globe.

4) [dwhitman] 10-3-2008(34): Bugs Bunny watches the sun go down at nightfall. "Eeeeh…That's all folks!".

5) [radical_tyro] 20-7-2007(9): Mr. Rabbit watches the sun go down. Nightfall is coming and he hasn't made it to the queen's yet!
