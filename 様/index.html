Esq.

Reading:

On-Yomi: ヨウ、ショウ — Kun-Yomi: さま、さん

Heisig story:

The abbreviation Esq. will help associate this character with the honorific form of address to which it belongs. Its elements are: tree . . . sheep . . . grains of rice. Note that the final vertical stroke in the element for sheep is extended to form the first stroke for grains of rice.
