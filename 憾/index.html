remorse

Reading:

On-Yomi: カン — Kun-Yomi: うら.む

Heisig story:

State of mind . . . emotion. Hint: the etymology of "remorse" indicates a memory that returns again and again to "bite at" one's conscience and disturb one's peace of mind.
