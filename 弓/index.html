Reading:

On-Yomi: キュウ — Kun-Yomi: ゆみ

Heisig story:

This character pictures the bent wooden bow. Later we will learn how to make the bowstring that goes with it (Frame 1490). If you stretch this character out and see the indentation on the left as its handle, the pictography should be clearer.
