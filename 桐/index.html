paulownia

Reading:

On-Yomi: トウ、ドウ — Kun-Yomi: きり

Heisig story:

Since you probably don't know what a paulownia tree is, we shall let the key word suggest the phrase "the Little Brothers of St. Paulownia." It is a short step to associate the tree with the monks to its right. (For the curious, the name of this oriental tree really comes from a Russian princess, Anna Pavlovna.)
