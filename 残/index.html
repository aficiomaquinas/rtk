remainder

Reading:

On-Yomi: ザン、サン — Kun-Yomi: のこ.る、のこ.す、そこな.う、のこ.り

Primitive:

bone This character is meant to be a pictograph of a bone attached to a piece of flesh (or vice versa.) The first stroke serves to keep it distinct from the character for evening (FRAME 109 夕). [4]

Heisig story:

Bones . . . (parade) float.
