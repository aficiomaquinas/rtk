pity

Reading:

On-Yomi: セキ — Kun-Yomi: お.しい、お.しむ

Heisig story:

State of mind . . . once upon a time. The sense of the key word is that of a lost opportunity or bad turn of affairs, as in the phrase "What a pity!"
