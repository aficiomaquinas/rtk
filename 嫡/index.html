Reading:

On-Yomi: チャク、テキ

Primitive:

antique(antyk, zabytek) The primitive meaning antique, not itself a kanji, depicts a vase kept under a glass hood because it is very, very old. [11]

The phrase legitimate wife would have no meaning if there were not such things as "illegitimate wives," taken because one's legal woman has turned into an antique. The very offense of the idea should help you remember the kanji.
