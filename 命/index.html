fate

Reading:

On-Yomi: メイ、ミョウ — Kun-Yomi: いのち

Heisig story:

This character connotes life in general, but also the particular life that is fated one by virtue of the distinctive character with which one is born. Its elements are: fit . . . stamp. The bottom portion of fit is nudged to the left in order to make room for the stamp.
