evening

Reading:

On-Yomi: セキ — Kun-Yomi: ゆう

Heisig story:

Just as the word evening adds a touch of formality or romanticism to the ordinary word "night," so the kanji for evening takes the ordinary looking moon in the night sky and has a cloud pass over it (as we saw in the last frame).

Heisig comment:

* The primitive keeps the same meaning and connotation as the kanji.
