ring

Reading:

On-Yomi: カン — Kun-Yomi: わ

Heisig story:

Jewel . . . eye . . . ceiling . . . mouth . . . scarf. The number of elements is large here, so take extra care. Learn it in conjunction with the next frame, since these are the only two cases in this book where the combination of elements to the right appears.
