gentleman

Reading:

On-Yomi: シ

Heisig story:

The shape of this kanji, slightly differing from that for soil by virtue of its shorter final stroke, hints at a broad-shouldered, slender-waisted warrior standing at attention. When feudalism collapsed, these warriors became Japan's gentlemen.

Heisig comment:

* The primitive meaning reverts to the more colorful image of the samurai, Japan's warrior class.
