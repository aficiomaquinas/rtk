obey

Reading:

On-Yomi: ジュン

Heisig story:

In primitive language, this character would read stream . . . head. And that turns out to be convenient for remembering its meaning of obey. Either one obeys the person who is head of an organization or else obeys by following the stream of opinion ("current" practice, we call it). Both these senses come together in this kanji.
