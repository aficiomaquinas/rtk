not

Reading:

On-Yomi: モチ、ブツ、ボツ — Kun-Yomi: なか.れ、なし

Heisig story:

First take the primitive meaning of this character: knot. Think of it as the piglet minus its body (the horizontal stroke), that is, the curly tail that looks like a knot. As an exception, we will use the homonym to remember the abstract key word, not.
