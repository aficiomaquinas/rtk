Heisig story:

Cloak . . . fruit.

Koohii stories:

3) [jmadsen] 13-2-2006(29): Eve was naked until she ate of the fruit and put a cloak on.

4) [Raichu] 14-1-2006(20): A cloak hides the fruit-bearing parts of one's naked body.

5) [kanjihito] 26-10-2012(18): The cloak slipped to her side to reveal her naked fruit (don't confuse with undress (#498 脱)).
