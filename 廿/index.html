twenty

Reading:

On-Yomi: ジュウ、ニュウ — Kun-Yomi: にじゅう

Heisig story:

The two tens joined at the bottom by a short line is actually the old character for twenty, which we might as well learn since we need its primitive form. It is written the same as salad, except for the shorter final stroke.
