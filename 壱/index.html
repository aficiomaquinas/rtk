I (one)

Reading:

On-Yomi: イチ、イツ — Kun-Yomi: ひとつ

Heisig story:

The Roman numeral I - like that for II we met earlier in frame 379 - is only rarely used now. In the midst of all the samurai, we notice one in particular sitting on the ground with a crown on his head, indicating that he is "number I" in the current rankings.
