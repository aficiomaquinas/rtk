## Heisig story:

And like the Roman numeral III, which triples the numeral I, the kanji for <b>three</b> simply triples the single horizontal stroke. In writing it, think of &quot;1 + 2 = 3&quot; (一 + 二&nbsp;= 三) in order to keep the middle stroke shorter.
