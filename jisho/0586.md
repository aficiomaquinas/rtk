## Heisig story:

The suffix &quot;<b>-times</b>&quot; refers to a number of repetitions. Its elements: <i>a mouth</i>&nbsp;. . . <i>pent in</i>. Hint: you may find it more helpful to forget the primitives and think of one circle revovling inside of another.
