## Heisig story:

Of all of nature's <b>disasters</b>, this kanji picks out two of the worst: <i>floods</i> and <i>fires</i>. To recall the disposition of the elements, think of nature's solution to nature's own problem: a great <i>flood</i> pouring down over a great forest <i>fire</i>.
