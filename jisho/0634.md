## Reading:

### On-Yomi: テン &mdash; Kun-Yomi: そ.える、そ.う、も.える、も.う

### Examples: 添う (そ.う), 添える (そ.える)

## Words:

添う(そう): to accompany, to become married, to comply with

添える(そえる): to add to, to attach, to append, to accompany, to garnish, to imitate, to annex
