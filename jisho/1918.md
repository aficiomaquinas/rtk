その為(そのため): hence, for that reason

為さる(なさる): to do

為替(かわせ): money order, exchange

為る(する): do, try, play, practice, cost, serve as, pass, elapse

所為(せい): act, deed, one´s doing

為(ため): for, in order to

為す(なす): accomplish, do
