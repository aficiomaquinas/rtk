## Reading:

### On-Yomi: カ &mdash; Kun-Yomi: よめ、とつ.ぐ、い.く、ゆ.く

## Heisig story:

The kanji in this frame demonstrates the traditional Japanese approach to marriage: it is the <i>woman</i> who leaves her family for another <i>house</i>hold, thus <b>marrying into</b> a man's family.
