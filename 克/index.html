overcome

Reading:

On-Yomi: コク — Kun-Yomi: か.つ

Heisig story:

In this frame we get a chance to use the kanji we just learned in its primitive meaning of teenager. The needle on top indicates one of the major problems confronting the teenager growing up in today's world: drugs. Many of them will fall under the shadow of the needle at some time during those tender years, but only when a whole generation rises up and decides that "We Shall Overcome" the plague, will the needle cease to hangover their heads, as it does in this character.
