On-Yomi: ショウ

Koohii stories:

1) [shirahime] 17-1-2008(41): Someone tries to lay their fingers on your spoils, and you run after them hitting them with a broom as they run from you.

2) [Katsuo] 28-5-2007(11): As my share of the spoils of war I grab a running-broom that has fingers (just like the one in Disney/Mickey Mouse/Fantasia). It runs round cleaning up with its broom and uses its fingers to press buttons etc and operate household appliances. But also like Mickey’s it gets out of control and spoils everything it touches. So much for the spoils of war.

3) [astridtops] 4-3-2007(5): Every morning, the street cleaners are running with their brooms, to pick up the spoils of their profession up with their fingers.

4) [dancingblade] 12-7-2010(3): Nimble: In my fingers I equip my Broom of Running. It can't fly, but it gives me +5 nimbleness. Now to quickly do some housework. NB: I couldn't find 'spoils' for the meaning for this kanji, so the next best thing was nimble which covers the meanings of fast and smart.

5) [Meconium] 20-10-2009(3): A mean old man leaves a bunch of rakes out on the footpath, so that when early morning joggers, trying to get some bleary-eyed exercise in at dawn, run past, they step on a rake and smack themselves in the head, knocking them out. The mean old man then comes out, cackling evilly, and reaches his fingers into their pockets, trying to steal their wallet, keys and whatever other spoils of some value they might be carrying.
