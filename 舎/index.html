cottage

Reading:

On-Yomi: シャ、セキ — Kun-Yomi: やど.る

Primitive:

lidded crock(garnek/zbanek z wiekiem) Soil over the mouth of a container gives us a piece of clay pottery with its lid. Behold the lidded crock. [6]

Heisig story:

A lidded crock with an umbrella overhead gives us a mixture of the modern and the nostalgic in this design for a cottage.
