When Venom realized he couldn't beat Spider-Man in a fair fight, he showed his cleverness by spraying venom all over Spider-Man's luxuriant garden.

2) [nest0r] 26-1-2008(52): Venom is covered in a luxuriant and surprisingly clever symbiotic suit, but it's also the taskmaster for his every evil act.

3) [aircawn] 31-12-2006(43): Every taskmaster's threads are luxuriant, made with only the best fabrics in all the brightest colours.

4) [spleenlol] 7-6-2010(38): This kanji is 1337 ^^.
