or again

Reading:

On-Yomi: ユウ — Kun-Yomi: また、また-、また.の-

Heisig story:

Like the several abbreviations in Roman script to indicate "and" (+, &, etc.), this short two-stroke kanji is used for the similar meaning of or again.

Heisig comment:

* As a primitive, it will mean crotch, as in the crotch of the arm. Or whatever.
