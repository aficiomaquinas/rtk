tall

Reading:

On-Yomi: コウ — Kun-Yomi: たか.い、たか、-だか、たか.まる、たか.める

Heisig story:

Recalling an image from frame 195, first see the mouth under the extraterrestrial's glass hood, and then the mouth under the top hat of one of his mates who has tried on the strange earthling's headgear only to find that it makes him look much, much taller than everyone else.

Heisig comment:

* As a primitive, this character keeps its sense of tall and its position at the top of other primitives, but its writing is abbreviated to the first 5 strokes.
