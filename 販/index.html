marketing

Reading:

On-Yomi: ハン

Heisig story:

Shells/money . . . anti-.

Koohii stories:

1) [genbaku] 9-1-2008(272): The goal of marketing is to get people who are naturally anti-buying to spend their shells (money).

2) [sutebun] 18-10-2007(54): 『MARKETING: MONEY, ANTI』 The wide presence of MARKETING in our society has led people to become ANTI-MONEY.

3) [Doodsaq] 17-12-2007(31): Dirty Harry (our anti-hero) refused the villainous marketing director's offer of money before he kicked him in the crotch and pushed him off the cliff.

4) [raulir] 28-4-2006(30): Marketing is an anti-money of sorts. When it comes into contact with money, the money disappears.

5) [calittle] 30-9-2007(18): Marketing tries to get consumers to be anti-money and pro-products.
