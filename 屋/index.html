roof

Reading:

On-Yomi: オク — Kun-Yomi: や

Heisig story:

Flag . . . climax. Note that this kanji has no relation to the drawing of a "roof" used in the primitive for house.

Koohii stories:

1) [samuize] 27-7-2007(219): Flag at the climax (highest point) of a castle roof.

2) [akrodha] 10-4-2007(113): A couple climaxing on the roof, draped in the American flag. The neighbors are mortified and call the landlady…

3) [Shibo] 13-5-2008(41): My girlfriend is crazy. First she drags me up onto the roof of her apartment building to have sex, then she wants me to pee all over her after I climax. She's crazy, but I love her! (Note: I use "pee" for the "flag" primitive).

4) [RoboTact] 4-3-2007(40): See room (#754 室). Room is roof + walls + soil (floor). Here roof is marked by flag to indicate what is being singled out in this kanji.

5) [elmaestrokgb] 10-6-2009(14): In the climax of Rush Hour, Jackie Chan slides down a giant flag (a kanji flag, no less) from the roof.
