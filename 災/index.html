On-Yomi: サイ — Kun-Yomi: わざわ.い

Heisig story:

Of all of nature's disasters, this kanji picks out two of the worst: floods and fires. To recall the disposition of the elements, think of nature's solution to nature's own problem: a great flood pouring down over a great forest fire.
