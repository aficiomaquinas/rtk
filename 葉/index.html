Reading:

On-Yomi: ヨウ — Kun-Yomi: は

Heisig story:

Three elements are given here: flower . . . generation . . . tree. The first and last seem logical enough, since it is the leaf that feeds the flowers on a tree. The element for generation interposed between the two suggests that the movement of a tree from one generation to the next is like its "turning over a new leaf."
