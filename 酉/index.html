sign of the bird

Reading:

On-Yomi: ユウ — Kun-Yomi: とり

Heisig story:

Though we shall later encounter the kanji for bird, we introduce this one for the tenth sign of the zodiac mainly because of its use as a primitive, where it has a different meaning.

Heisig comment:

* As a primitive, it means whiskey bottle. In its pictograph, you can see the loosely corked lid, the bottle and the contents (about one-third full). You might also think of the Spanish "porrón," a decanter shaped like a long-necked bird.
