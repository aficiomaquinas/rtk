renunciation

Reading:

On-Yomi: テイ、タイ — Kun-Yomi: あき.らめる、あきら.める、つまびらか、まこと

Heisig story:

The key word renunciation has to do with the wisdom and clarity of mind in knowing when to "let go." This is what makes the words of acquiescense sovern.
