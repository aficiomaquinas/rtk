co-

Reading:

On-Yomi: キョウ

Heisig story:

This prefix should be kept distinct from inter (Frame 222) and mutual (Frame 819). Its elements: needle . . . triceps.

Koohii stories:

1) [fuaburisu] 7-2-2006(321): The nurse says : “If you co-operate, that needle (syringe) in your triceps won't hurt”. Note: both co- and cooperate are meanings for this kanji.

2) [xxinde] 21-4-2009(103): Co-operation: 3 persons can have the power of ten if they co-operate.

3) [laner36] 17-3-2008(22): 3 times Arnold (Schwazzaneggar) asked his co-workers to cooperate and give him a needle (syringe) of steroids (into his tricep), but each time they refused to co-ooperate.

4) [Danieru] 23-6-2008(21): The nurse says: "If you don't co-operate with us and let us stick this needle into your triceps, sir, we'll have to stick it somewhere else! (Thnx Fabrice!).

5) [axelb] 11-8-2008(11): If we cooperate, we will have ten times as much power and triceps.
