depression

Reading:

On-Yomi: ワ、ア — Kun-Yomi: くぼ.む、くぼ.み、くぼ.まる、くぼ

Heisig story:

Hole . . . water . . . ivy. The depression referred to here is a sunken place in the ground, rather than in one's spirits.
