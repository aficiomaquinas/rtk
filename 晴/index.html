clear up

Reading:

On-Yomi: セイ — Kun-Yomi: は.れる、は.れ、は.れ-、-ば.れ、は.らす

Heisig story:

Take the key word in its associations with the weather (unless that tempts you to include the primitive for weather, which doesn't belong here). Its elements: sun . . . blue.

Koohii stories:

1) [Raichu] 22-2-2006(378): Sun and blue sky! Yep, the weather's cleared up!

2) [dilandau23] 1-4-2007(25): When we speak of clearing up we easily think of both the sun and blue skies. But in another since to clear up is the absence of weather. And therefor the reason weather has no place in its writing.

3) [andresito] 17-5-2009(17): The sky cleared up and we have a sunny blue day.

4) [Filip] 10-2-2008(9): Its clear weather now. Smufs are sunbathing. Sun and blue sky. What more do they need?

5) [blackstockc] 16-1-2008(3): Once it clears up, the sun will wash your blues away!
