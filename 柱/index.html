pillar

Reading:

On-Yomi: チュウ — Kun-Yomi: はしら

Heisig story:

The pillar referred to here is the wooden beam that stands at the entrance to a traditional Japanese house. Carve it in imagination into the shape of a gigantic candlestick and your work is done.
