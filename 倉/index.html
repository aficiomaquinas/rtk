godown

Reading:

On-Yomi: ソウ — Kun-Yomi: くら

Heisig story:

The single gate is used here not in order to represent one gate, but many of them, indeed a meeting of gates. Add mouth (as an entrance here) and you end up with godown. That should help keep this character distinct from warehouse (Frame 633).
