nose

Reading:

On-Yomi: ビ — Kun-Yomi: はな

Heisig story:

Let me share a rather grotesque image to help with this kanji. Imagine taking your two hands and reaching up into someone's nostrils. Once inside you grab hold of the brain and yank it out. At the end, you would have a picture something like that of this character, the full kanji for nose.
