test

Reading:

On-Yomi: シ — Kun-Yomi: こころ.みる、ため.す

Heisig story:

When a manufacturer produces a new style for the market, the first thing that is done is to run a test on consumers, asking them to speak their opinions frankly about the product. Never mind the anachronism (the kanji was there well before our capitalistic market system) if it helps you remember.
