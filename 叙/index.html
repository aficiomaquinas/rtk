Reading:

On-Yomi: ジョ — Kun-Yomi: つい.ず、ついで

Heisig story:

Scale . . crotch. The key word has to do with conferring ranks, titles, and awards. It should not be confused with bestow (Frame 1335) or impart (Frame 795).
