rapidly

Reading:

On-Yomi: シツ — Kun-Yomi: はや.い

Heisig story:

Be sure to keep this character distinct from quick (Frame 1799) and swift (Frame 298). Picture a succession of poison darts (the sort that inflict sickness) flying out rapid-fire from a blowgun, so that "rapid-fire" can conjure up the proper image.
