grains of sand

Reading:

On-Yomi: サ、シャ — Kun-Yomi: すな、よなげる

Heisig story:

We have already learned the kanji for sand (Frame 122), so let's use it to remember the character for grains of sand. Instead of the "few stones" that make for nice sand, here we have a few drops of water, one for each grand of sand - a beach in perfect ecological balance.
