defile

Heisig story:

Water . . . precious.

STORY: Use PRECIOUS WATER (i.e. holy water) to BREAK/SMASH the face of the undead. STORY 2: If you SMASH something in heaven, you can just pour some PRECIOUS WATER (holy water) on it to fix it.
