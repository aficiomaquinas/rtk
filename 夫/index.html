husband

Reading:

On-Yomi: フ、フウ、ブ — Kun-Yomi: おっと、そ.れ

Heisig story:

The kanji for a husband or "head of the family" is based on the kanji for large and an extra line near the top for the "head." Recall the kanji for heavens already learned back in frame 457, and be sure to keep your story for this kanji different.
