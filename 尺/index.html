shaku

Reading:

On-Yomi: シャク

Heisig story:

The key word shaku has actually come into English in the word shakuhachi, the ancient Japanese flute that measured "one shaku and eight sun" (the "sun" being about an inch in length). Since the shaku is about one foot in length, this makes about 20 inches. Let the final sweeping stroke be like a tape measure added to the flag.

Heisig comment:

* As a primitive, this will mean the shakuhachi flute.
